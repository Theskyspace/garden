Self-imposed challenge: take an exam-prep idea from zero to live on the Play Store in
two weeks, learning Android Studio along the way. The product itself is straightforward
— question banks, mock tests, performance tracking — but the constraint was the point.

It became a personal proof: a clean two-week sprint, with no prior platform knowledge,
ends with something real users can install. I refer back to that timeline whenever a
project starts to feel impossible.
